The SafeDT Project Has Officially Started!

01-10-2025

Dr Alejandro Jiménez Rios has been awarded a 2025 CoSeC Fellowship to support the SafeDT project: Safe Digital Twins for the Built Environment.

The project officially began on 1 October 2025 and will run until 31 December 2026. Through this Fellowship, SafeDT will advance the development of secure, resilient, and trustworthy digital twins for heritage and modern infrastructure, working in close collaboration with experts across the CoSeC and STFC communities.

Further updates, including details of upcoming events and project outputs, will be shared on this website throughout the Fellowship period.




SafeDT Presented at the 2025 CoSeC and CIUK Conferences

06-12-2025

In December 2025, Dr Alejandro Jiménez Rios attended both the 2025 CoSeC Conference and the 2025 CIUK Conference in Manchester, UK, as part of his CoSeC Fellowship.

These events provided an excellent opportunity to engage with the UK's computational science community, explore emerging advances in cybersecurity, HPC, and software sustainability, and discuss the SafeDT project's focus on the safe and responsible deployment of digital twins for the built environment.

Insights gained during the conferences will directly inform ongoing SafeDT activities and future collaborations with CoSeC and STFC UKRI partners. Further updates will be shared as the project progresses.
